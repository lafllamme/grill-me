## Observed system

- Long content is grouped into large rounded section shells, commonly `28-64px` at macro level.
- Inner modules step down to `12-24px`.
- The page alternates clean white/gray shells with high-energy visual texture rather than using a single card style everywhere.
- Bento layouts are asymmetric and mix people, product images, metrics, and text.
- Each shell has one clear job and generous internal space.

## Why it matters

Awesomic gives the clearest radius hierarchy and demonstrates that rounded section architecture can organize a very long page without feeling childish.

## Grillme translation

- Use a few large rounded chapter shells instead of opaque full-width rectangles.
- Mix roast proof, commit excerpts, and metrics in asymmetric bento compositions.
- Keep inner radii visibly tighter than outer shells.
- Let the Prism background appear in the gutters between shells.

## Behavior and extractable components

- Large shells group related information; internal modules use visibly smaller radii and tighter spacing.
- Uneven bento spans reflect content importance rather than filling a symmetric template.
- Extract one evidence bento with three jobs: source receipts, intensity control, and shareable result. Avoid a generic feature list.
