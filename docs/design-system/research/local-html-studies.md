## Rounded layout sample

File: `/Users/flame/Downloads/beispieLrunder.html`

This sample is brighter and less relevant to the dark Grillme direction, but it demonstrates a useful distinction:

- pill navigation and controls can coexist with mostly rectangular editorial sections
- rounded controls alone do not create a rounded design language
- generous whitespace and clear section headings matter more than adding radius everywhere

Its orange should not influence the rebrand palette.

## Bordeaux sample

File: `/Users/flame/Downloads/beispiel_bordeaux.html`

The sample uses a classic bordeaux around `#651C24` on white. It confirms that bordeaux can feel premium and calm, but the execution is too corporate and light for Grillme.

Transferable:

- deep red as a contained panel and CTA color
- off-white copy and muted rose companions
- strong contrast without neon saturation

Not transferable:

- consulting-agency layout
- large white page fields
- conservative equal card row

The useful palette lesson is to combine a deep wine anchor with a brighter signal red, rather than asking one red to perform every role.
